What to Include in a Support Request

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if available

Getting Sound

GrimoHex is a standalone sampler and sequencer. No external hardware is required.

To produce sound:

  1. Launch the app
  2. Tap any trigger pad (T1–T8)
  3. Ensure your device is not muted and volume is up

Bluetooth headphones may introduce latency. Wired headphones or built-in speakers are better for timing-sensitive use. MIDI devices are optional and not required for sound.

Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

Does GrimoHex require external hardware?

No. GrimoHex is fully standalone. External MIDI or audio hardware is optional.

Why is there no sound?

Check device mute switch, volume level, audio output route, and make sure you are tapping a trigger pad.

Why is Bluetooth audio delayed?

Bluetooth can add latency. For accurate timing, use wired headphones, built-in speakers, or a low-latency audio interface.

My MIDI device is not detected. What should I check?

Confirm the device is class-compliant, connected through the correct adapter, powered if needed, and selected in the app.

How do I report a bug?

Email support@grimohex.com with device, OS version, app version, and reproduction steps.
