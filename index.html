GrimoHex

A tactile sampler and beat sequencer for iPhone and iPad.

GrimoHex is a mobile sampler and performance sequencer for creating beats, recording or importing samples, sequencing patterns, shaping sound, and getting ideas moving quickly.

Available on the App Store Visit Support

GrimoHex is a standalone sampler and sequencer. No external hardware is required. MIDI and audio hardware can expand the workflow, but they are optional.
