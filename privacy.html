Privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how GrimoHex handles data, permissions, audio, files, and connected music devices.

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Introduction

GrimoHex is an iOS and iPadOS music creation app for sample-based beatmaking, sequencing, and performance. The app is designed to run locally on your device.

Data Collection

GrimoHex does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Audio recorded or imported by the user remains on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export or share it.

GrimoHex does not currently use third-party analytics or crash reporting tools.

Data Usage

Any audio, project, or performance data created in GrimoHex is used to provide app functionality such as sample playback, sequencing, sound shaping, recording, importing, and exporting when the user chooses to do so.

Microphone, Files, Audio, and MIDI

GrimoHex may request microphone access to record samples. GrimoHex may request file access when the user chooses to import audio. GrimoHex may interact with MIDI/audio devices when the user connects them.

These permissions are used only to provide app functionality.

Data Sharing

GrimoHex does not sell personal data and does not share personal data with third parties. User-created audio is shared only when the user chooses to export, save, send, or otherwise share it outside the app.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to support@grimohex.com.
